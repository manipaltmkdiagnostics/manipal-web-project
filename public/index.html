❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a home sample collection? +

You can book a home sample collection by calling us at +91 99800 80433 or by adding tests to your cart on our website and submitting your details. Our phlebotomist will visit at your convenience.

How soon will I receive my test reports? +

Most routine test reports are available within 6-12 hours. Specialized tests may take up to 24-48 hours. You'll receive your reports via email and SMS as soon as they are ready.

Do I need to fast before blood tests? +

Some tests like Lipid Profile, Blood Sugar Fasting, and Liver Function Tests require 10-12 hours of overnight fasting. Each test listing on our website mentions whether fasting is required.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept cash, all major credit/debit cards, UPI payments (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), and net banking. Payment can be made at the centre or at the time of home collection.

Can I walk in without an appointment? +

Absolutely! Walk-ins are welcome during our operating hours (Mon-Sat, 7:00 AM - 9:00 PM). However, booking in advance helps us serve you faster and with less waiting time.
